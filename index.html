I'm Jazelle

A digital designer located in 's-Hertogenbosch, Netherlands.

Prior to UX/UI design, I worked in e-commerce as a logistics and customer support specialist. Most recently I helped develop and design PostReview.

As a designer, I'm passionate about creating designs that can leave a lasting and meaningful impact on people's lives.

As a human, I'm usually spotted taking photos, smelling flowers, watching birds, and cuddling our cat.

More than anything, I thrive amidst aspiration, zealous curiosity, ease, and bliss.

I like to...

Do the research

Determine the client's needs, understand the users, and visualize potential solutions that emphasize empathy.

Think holistically

Establish usability, meet accessibility needs, and design with sustainability in mind. Listen to people, always.

Keep things simple

Define unique solutions while keeping things as simple and intuitive as possible. The best solution is the one that is uncomplicated.
